Domain 1 – Security and Risk Management

1An e-commerce company processes 600,000 Visa transactions annually and accepts card payments directly (not through a hosted payment form). Under PCI-DSS, which merchant level applies?
✅ Correct: C. PCI-DSS Merchant Level 3 applies to e-commerce merchants processing 20,001–1,000,000 Visa e-commerce transactions per year. 600,000 falls squarely in this range. Level 2 covers 1–6 million transactions. The channel (e-commerce) matters: a brick-and-mortar merchant with 600,000 transactions has different level rules than an e-commerce merchant.
2Under the HIPAA Security Rule, which of the following is an ADDRESSABLE implementation specification (not a "required" one)?
✅ Correct: C. Automatic logoff (§164.312(a)(2)(iii)) is an addressable specification — covered entities must assess whether it is reasonable and appropriate and either implement it or document why an equivalent alternative was used. Unique user identification, emergency access procedures, and person/entity authentication are all "required" implementation specifications that must be implemented as-is. This distinction is heavily tested on the exam.
3SOX Section 404 requires which of the following?
✅ Correct: B. SOX Section 404 requires management to assess and report on the effectiveness of internal controls over financial reporting, with the external auditor providing an attestation. Option A describes Section 906 (criminal penalties). Option C describes Section 409 (real-time disclosure). Option D describes Section 802 (document retention). Knowing which section covers which requirement is a common exam question format.
4A financial services company is subject to both PCI-DSS and GLBA. The GLBA Safeguards Rule requires stronger encryption standards in several areas than PCI-DSS requires. What is the MOST appropriate course of action?
✅ Correct: B. When multiple regulatory frameworks apply, an organization must meet the MOST stringent applicable requirement. Meeting a higher standard than the minimum is always acceptable. There is no mechanism to "opt out" of overlapping regulations. Creating silos may not be technically feasible and doesn't eliminate regulatory exposure. This is a key compliance principle for organizations in regulated industries.
5A healthcare organization discovers a breach of unsecured PHI on a Monday. The breach occurred the prior Friday evening. Which of the following CORRECTLY states the applicable notification requirements?
✅ Correct: B. This is one of the most frequently tested distinctions on the CISSP exam. HIPAA Breach Notification Rule: covered entities must notify HHS and affected individuals within 60 calendar days of discovery. GDPR Article 33: notification to the supervisory authority must be made within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach. Never swap these — "72 hours = GDPR; 60 days = HIPAA."
6PCI-DSS Requirement 8 specifically addresses which security domain?
✅ Correct: C. PCI-DSS Requirement 8 = Identify and authenticate access to system components (user IDs, passwords, MFA). Option A = Requirement 11 (test security). Option B = Requirement 9 (physical access). Option D = Requirement 12 (security policy). Being able to match PCI-DSS requirement numbers to their subject areas is a tested skill — focus especially on requirements 6 (secure systems), 8 (authentication), 10 (logging), 11 (testing).
7During a PCI-DSS assessment, a QSA discovers that an organization cannot technically implement a required control due to a legacy system constraint. The organization has implemented alternative controls that appear to meet the security intent of the original requirement. What is the MOST appropriate outcome under PCI-DSS?
✅ Correct: B. PCI-DSS explicitly allows compensating controls when a technical or business constraint legitimately prevents implementing the standard requirement. The organization must document the constraint, the compensating controls in a Compensating Control Worksheet, and demonstrate that the alternatives meet the intended security objective with equivalent rigor. The QSA validates this independently — it does not require payment brand waiver approval.
8A publicly traded company outsources its payroll processing to a cloud service provider. The payroll data is material to financial reporting accuracy. Under SOX, which statement MOST accurately describes the cloud provider's security obligations?
✅ Correct: B. SOX compliance responsibility follows where financially material data flows. A public company cannot escape SOX obligations by outsourcing. The company must contractually require its service providers to maintain controls that support SOX compliance, and failures in those controls affect the company's annual controls assessment. This is why SOC 1 Type II reports are requested from vendors processing financially material data.
9A university receives a subpoena for student educational records. Under FERPA, the MOST appropriate action before complying is:
✅ Correct: C. FERPA requires educational institutions to make a reasonable attempt to notify the student (or parent, if applicable) before complying with a lawfully issued subpoena, allowing the student an opportunity to seek a protective order. The exception is when the subpoena specifically prohibits such notification (e.g., in a law enforcement context). FERPA does not give students absolute veto power over lawful legal process.
10An organization has deployed all technical controls required by applicable regulations but has no documented compliance monitoring or review program. What risk does this MOST directly create?
✅ Correct: B. Compliance is a continuous process, not a one-time certification event. Without ongoing monitoring, controls degrade through configuration drift, system changes, or personnel turnover. Regulators and auditors require evidence of continuous compliance — point-in-time implementation without monitoring is insufficient. Option D is dangerously wrong: technical controls fail, are misconfigured, or are bypassed regularly — monitoring is essential.
