2. Investigations & Digital Forensics (Days 101-103)

Investigation Types Quick Reference

TypeBurden of ProofStandardOutcome
CriminalProsecutionBeyond a reasonable doubt (highest)Imprisonment, fines
CivilPlaintiffPreponderance of evidence (more likely than not)Monetary damages
Administrative (internal)OrganizationSubstantial evidence / policy violationTermination, sanctions
RegulatoryRegulatorVaries by jurisdictionFines, license revocation

Evidence Admissibility — RRSLA

🧠 RRSLA: Relevant, Reliable, Sufficient, Legally obtained, Authentic

Forensic Process — NIST SP 800-86 (CEAR)

🧠 CEAR: Collection → Examination → Analysis → Reporting

Key Evidence Concepts

  • Best evidence rule: Original documents preferred over copies
  • Chain of custody: Documented handling from collection to court (WWW-HAT: Who, What, When, How, Action, Transfer)
  • Order of volatility: Collect most volatile first: registers → cache → RAM → disk → removable → logs → archives
  • Write blockers: Required to prevent modification during forensic imaging
  • Dual hashing: MD5 + SHA-256 for integrity verification

8. Commonly Confused Concepts

Concept AConcept BThe Difference
BCPDRPBCP = business-wide continuity (umbrella); DRP = IT recovery specifically (subset)
RTORPORTO = time to restore systems (forward looking); RPO = max data loss (backward looking)
IDSIPSIDS = detective (alerts only); IPS = preventive (blocks traffic)
IoCIoAIoC = evidence attack happened (reactive); IoA = behavior suggesting attack in progress (proactive)
IncrementalDifferentialIncremental clears archive bit (since last backup); Differential doesn't (since last full)
Parallel testFull interruptionParallel = primary stays running; Full = primary shut down
SIEMSOARSIEM = correlate/alert (detective); SOAR = automate response (responsive)
EnticementEntrapmentEnticement = legal (opportunity for willing offender); Entrapment = illegal (inducing someone who wouldn't otherwise commit)
Sync replicationAsync replicationSync = RPO 0, distance-limited; Async = RPO>0, unlimited distance

9. High-Frequency Exam Topics

Top 10 Most-Tested Domain 7 Concepts:
  1. DR test types — order from least to most disruptive
  2. RTO vs RPO vs MTD — definitions and relationships
  3. Hot/Warm/Cold sites — features, cost, activation time
  4. Backup types — full/incremental/differential and archive bit behavior
  5. RAID levels — 0, 1, 5, 10 particularly
  6. Fire suppression — FM-200, Halon, CO₂, pre-action sprinklers
  7. Chain of custody — evidence handling requirements
  8. IR lifecycle phases — NIST 800-61 order and key activities
  9. Evidence admissibility — types and standards
  10. Human safety priority — always #1 in any scenario
